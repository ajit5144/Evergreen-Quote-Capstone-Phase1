Understanding Your Coverage

Explore liability and renters coverage options designed to help protect you and your belongings.

What is Personal Liability Coverage?

Personal Liability Coverage is an important part of a renters insurance policy that helps protect you financially if you are found legally responsible for causing bodily injury to someone else or damage to their property.

For example, if a visitor is injured in your apartment and you are held responsible, or if you accidentally cause damage to a neighbor's property, Personal Liability Coverage may help pay for covered legal expenses, medical costs, or repair costs up to your policy limits.

Many landlords require tenants to carry liability coverage as part of a lease agreement because it provides an added layer of financial protection for both renters and property owners.

Benefits of Personal Liability Coverage

  • Helps satisfy landlord or lease requirements.
  • Provides financial protection against covered liability claims.
  • May help cover legal defense costs for covered incidents.
  • Offers peace of mind at an affordable cost.
  • Can be purchased as part of a broader renters insurance solution.
