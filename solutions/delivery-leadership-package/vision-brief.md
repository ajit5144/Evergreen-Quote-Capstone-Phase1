## Vision of Evergreen Insurance.
Create a mobile-first insurance quoting experience that enables renters to obtain a Personal Liability quote in under 2 minutes with a simple, transparent, and self-service experience.

## Who is the customer?
Renters who mainly want Personal Liability coverage, often to satisfy landlord or lease requirements, and who prefer a simple, low-cost, easy-to-buy insurance option.

## What is the Customer Pain Point?
Many renters:
- Need liability coverage to satisfy landlord requirements.
- Do not understand the difference between liability-only coverage and traditional renters insurance.
- View existing renters insurance quote journeys as lengthy and overly complex.
- Want immediate pricing without creating an account.

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
Evergreen Quote should provide a streamlined journey focused on Personal Liability coverage first, while allowing customers to optionally explore broader renters coverage without increasing complexity.

## What does "good" look like at end of the week?
- Accessible URL with a responsive experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- User can complete the quote flow and receive a premium estimate 
- Solution follows a PR-based development process with automated CI validation.

## What are we explicitly NOT doing this week?
- The premium calculation is a placeholder using a simplified calculation mechanism and is not intended to represent actual insurance pricing.
- No Policies or accounts will be created. Customer cannot text or email the premium quote
- Production-grade security, penetration testing, and vulnerability remediation are outside the scope of this MVP.

## How will we know if it worked?
- Stakeholders can complete the quote flow on a mobile device without horizontal scrolling.
- A premium estimate is displayed successfully for all test scenarios.
- The application is accessible via a public URL.
- The end-to-end demonstration can be completed without workarounds or manual intervention.
